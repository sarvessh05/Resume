### 4. Create Your First Job

1. Click "Create New Job"
2. Fill in:
   - Job Title: "Senior Frontend Developer"
   - Description: Brief job description
   - Experience: 3-7 years
   - Required Skills: React, TypeScript, JavaScript
   - Optional Skills: Next.js, GraphQL

3. Click "Create Job Opening"

### 5. Upload Resumes

1. Click on your job
2. Drag & drop PDF/DOCX resumes OR click browse
3. Watch AI analyze them in real-time!

### 6. View Results

- See match scores (0-100%)
- View candidate details
- Filter by score
- Export shortlisted candidates
